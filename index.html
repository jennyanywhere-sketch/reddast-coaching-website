Did you fall into your career and now feel stuck? It's time to design a fulfilling career.

We often launch our careers by taking the first opportunity that comes our way. Success follows and you progress within your company, industry or profession. Yet deep-down you feel something isn't quite right and eventually you find yourself unhappy, stuck and dissatisfied. Career coaching can reconnect you to your values and strengths, generate new ideas and opportunities, and put you back on a path to a fulfilling career.

Hello, I'm Jenny.

I'm an experienced career coach, licensed by Career Counselling Services, and have had a successful leadership career in tech. I now have a rewarding portfolio career combining consulting, coaching, volunteering and study.

I use career coaching techniques, design thinking and lived experience to help you reconcile your position, articulate your work philosophy and strengths, and find what energises and engages you. Together we will generate career options, map your career direction and focus on the action needed to uncover and explore opportunities.

Whether you decide to grow in your current role, pivot to a new industry or profession, pursue a passion project, start your own business, engage in your local community, or something else … the opportunities are endless, yet always unique to you.

My story

I've had a varied career as a tech leader across global corporates and start-ups. I've lived in the US and Spain and have a successful consulting company in the UK.

And yet I never really had a clear view of why I did my work each day. I moved from one job to another as opportunities popped up without much thought; I was fine just so long as I liked the people I worked with, was kept busy and could pay the bills.

Then life shifted, I was widowed unexpectedly and given a harsh reminder that life is fragile and our time here is precious.

I realised I wanted to be more purposeful in how I spent my time. I didn't want to return to an all-consuming role. Instead, I looked for opportunities aligned to my values that would use my strengths in ways I found energising and engaging. Through career counselling I gained clarity and figured out my direction.

Professional training as a licensed career counsellor and establishing a coaching practice followed; alongside post-graduate studies in psychology. I also reconnected with my local community through becoming an adviser at Citizens Advice and an ambassador for our local hospice. I've been able to prioritise my family and friends, build a coaching business, contribute locally and continue consulting.

I set up Reddast Coaching to empower you to reclaim your life and find a fulfilling career.

What's in the name?

The word Reddast comes from the Icelandic saying "Þetta Reddast", meaning that everything will work out fine. The Icelandic people have an uplifting combination of optimism, pragmatism and stoicism. Living in an unpredictable landscape, they have an unrelentingly belief that no matter how big a problem, the solution will always present itself. It's an ethos that runs through how we work with you at Reddast Coaching.

Programmes

Career Deep Dive

A structured framework to kick-start your career journey.

Exploring

A space to reflect on your career to date, your work values, interests, strengths and motivators. We'll use exercises and questionnaires to explore what makes you thrive and help you gain deeper insight.

Clarifying

A time to bring together the insights gained through exploration into a picture of what key elements will lead to a fulfilling career for you.

Visioning

Opening up your mind and imagining possible futures that could develop into possible career directions.

Action

A realistic assessment of the different options - identifying obstacles and how to overcome them. Planning practical next steps and sharing resources to enable you to research, network and develop opportunities.
